## AI Tells to Never Use

For the full list, see `.claude/agents/writing-validator.md`. Key patterns:

### Structural Patterns (Instant Flags)
- **Em dashes** (--): The #1 AI tell. Use periods or rewrite.
- **Two-word rhetorical questions**: "The catch?" "The truth?" "The answer?"
- **Tricolon (lists of three)**: "Not this, not that, just this."
- **Dramatic one-word sentences**: "Period." "Done." "Simple."
- **Faux casual openers**: "Here's the thing..." "Look, I get it..."

### Words to Never Use
- delve / dive into
- leverage
- utilize (just say "use")
- robust / seamless / cutting-edge
- game-changer / unlock / empower
